Monitoring the human scale of our health interventions across 12 active regions.

Amara K.

Eastern Province • Oct 12, 2023

Nutrition Focus
Life Before

Daily struggle to provide balanced meals. Energy levels were low, affecting seasonal harvesting capabilities across the small family plot.

Observed Changes

Stable weekly food supply secured. Amara reports a significant increase in physical stamina and weight gain in the younger household members.

"The support didn't just give us food; it gave us back our strength to work the land ourselves. Our morning table is full for the first time in years."

Lidya T.

Central Valley • Nov 05, 2023

Maternal Care
Life Before

Limited access to prenatal screenings. Travel to the nearest clinic was an 8-hour journey on foot, leading to missed critical check-ups.

Observed Changes

Mobile clinic visits every fortnight. Lidya received all 8 recommended WHO prenatal visits and delivered a healthy baby at the new hub.

"Seeing the mobile unit arrive in our village was like seeing hope on wheels. My son is thriving because the care came to us."

Samuel M.

Northern Highlands • Sep 28, 2023

Water Safety
Life Before

High absenteeism at the local primary school due to recurring water-borne illnesses among the student population and staff.

Observed Changes

Installation of 3 communal solar-powered filtration units. Illness-related absences have dropped by 74% in the last academic quarter.

"The classroom is finally full again. You don't realize how much the water holds you back until it is finally clear and safe."

Zoe Q.

Western District • Nov 18, 2023

Field Impact
Life Before

Manual data collection on paper led to weeks of lag between detecting a local health trend and deploying resources to address it.

Observed Changes

Real-time reporting via Silk Data tools allows for immediate logistics coordination. Resource wastage has been reduced by nearly 30%.

"The digital shift feels like gaining a new sense. We can see the needs of our community as they happen, not a month later."

Kwame B.

Southern Coast • Oct 30, 2023

Food Security
Life Before

High reliance on seasonal markets with volatile pricing, leading to intermittent food insecurity during lean months.

Observed Changes

Community-led grain storage initiative established. 100% of households now have a reserve that lasts through the dry season.

"The hunger months used to be a time of fear. Now, they are just another season. We have locked the door on uncertainty."
